TORTILLA SCRAMBLED EGG AND CHEESE

Easy and healthy Breakfast Recipe

A crispy tortilla, scrambled egg, and cheese! sounds like breakfast pizza to me!

This easy and healthy morning delicacy can be enjoyed with a delicious side of some toast and some good 'ol orange juice!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 (7 inch) flour tortilla
  • 2 slices jalapeno pepper jack cheese

STEPS

  1. Whisk egg and milk together in a small bowl
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour egg such that it covers the skillet.
  3. Cook until the eggs are set, which should be about 2 minutes in.
  4. After one side is almost done, place a tortilla and carefully flip the eggs on the other side.
  5. Place cheese slices over the eggs.
  6. Cook untill the tortilla is browned and the cheese is melted.
